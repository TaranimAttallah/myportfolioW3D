BLENDER

Blender is a versatile and open-source 3D computer graphics software used for creating 3D models, animations, visual effects, and more. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools for modeling, texturing, lighting, rendering, and animating 3D content. Blender is widely used by artists, animators, and designers for various purposes, including game development, film production, architectural visualization, and 3D art creation.

WEB DESIGN

Web design is the art and science of creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It involves combining elements like layout, color, typography, and imagery to craft a digital space that conveys information and engages visitors effectively. A well-designed website not only looks good but also functions seamlessly, offering an excellent user experience. Web designers consider factors like responsive design for various devices, accessibility, and the latest design trends. In essence, web design plays a crucial role in shaping a brand's online presence, making it more attractive and accessible to its audience.
